Feel like sweets but not much effort? This is the recipe for you!

Ingridients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Baking Powder
  • Two Sticks of Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs

Now to make the batter

Preperation

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Mix the dry ingridients in one bowl, and wet ingridients in another
  3. Gradually blend the flour with butter mixture
  4. Roll the dough into balls
  5. Place balls onto baking sheet, and bake for 8 to 10 minutes
  6. Let them cool on a baking rack, and enjoy!
