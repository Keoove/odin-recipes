Curry

You know when you eat something so good, you can't stop? Curry does that, it does that so well. Here's how to make it!

This is a japanese curry, so it might not taste the same as something in India, or the middle east, it's everywhere

Ingridients

Roux

  • 4 tbsp. butter
  • 4 tbsp. all-purpose flour
  • japanese curry powder

Curry

  • 2 lb boneless chicken breast and thighs
  • 2 tsp. neutral oil (vegtable or canola)
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 large russet potato
  • 1 1/2 cups carrot
  • 1/2 cup grated apple
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 3 1/2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp. tonkasu sauce

Directions

Roux

  1. Melt butter in small saucepan over medium heat, stir in flour,keep stirring and cook for about 20 minutes
  2. Add curry powder and stir for another minute, set aside.

Curry

  1. Heat Canola oil in 3 quart saucepan over medium-high heat, brown chicken on all sides.
  2. Reduce heat to medium, add onions, caramelize 5 to 10 minutes, add 1 tbsp. oil if too dry.
  3. Add potatoes, carrots, grated apple, mushrooms, and browned chicken to onions, pour water over, and stir.
  4. Add the roux, stir to dissolve, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Season to taste with Tonkatsu sauce, salt and pepper, and serve.
