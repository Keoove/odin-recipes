Lasagna

Have you ever wanted to NOT buy a frozen block of meat, sauce, and mashed plant goop? Well now you can make it with these simple steps!

Gather your ingridients

Ingridients

  • ground beef and sausage
  • two cloves of garlic
  • one onion
  • two cans tomato sauce
  • two cans tomato paste
  • one can crushed tomatoes
  • two tablespoons sugar
  • parsley, basil, salt, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • lasagna pasta
  • parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta cheese
  • an egg

Now time to put them together

Preperation Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Make the meat sauce
  3. Cook the noodles
  4. Make the ricotta mixture
  5. Layer the lasagna

Here's how to assemble the lasagna

Layering

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella Slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat, and end with top layer as Parmesan

Cover the Lasagna with aluminium foil, and bake it for 25 minutes. Uncover, and bake for 25 more minutes. Take it out, and let it rest for 15 minutes, and enjoy!
